Hire Senior Engineer India Fast: What 48-Hour Deployment Actually Requires

Speed is a process, not a promise. Here is what has to be true before day one.

  • Average deployment cycle: 48 hours vs industry 21 days
  • 97% technical screen pass rate on pre-vetted pool
  • Contract-first hiring reduces cost-per-hire by 34%

The problem isn't supply

Companies assume hiring slowly means the talent pool is thin. It rarely is. The bottleneck is process, not availability.

Why 48 hours is possible

A pre-vetted pool with active availability status removes the sourcing and screening steps entirely from the client timeline.

Speed without vetting is just risk with a deadline.
  • Pre-vet before you need to hire, not after
  • Contract-first reduces onboarding risk
  • Define scope before the search starts
