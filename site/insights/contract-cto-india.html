Contract CTO India: When Fractional Leadership Makes Sense

Not every startup needs a full-time CTO. Here is how to know which stage you're in.

Key findings

  • Fractional CTO engagements average 15-20 hours/week
  • Cost is typically 30-40% of a full-time senior hire
  • Most engagements convert to advisory role after 12 months

The problem isn't leadership, it's timing

Early-stage companies often hire a full-time CTO before the technical complexity justifies the cost.

Why fractional works at this stage

A contract CTO brings pattern recognition from multiple companies without the fixed cost of full-time equity and salary.

You don't need a permanent CTO. You need permanent clarity.

Key takeaways

  • Match engagement type to actual technical complexity
  • Fractional works best before Series A
  • Define a clear handoff plan from day one

Kavita Rai, Founder, MindMach
