Tech Talent Staffing India: What Changed After the Layoffs

5.8 million professionals moved. Most companies still haven't updated how they hire.

  • 5.8M tech professionals shifted roles post-layoffs
  • NASSCOM projects $315B market by 2027
  • SMBs now drive 40% of new engineering hiring demand

India does not have a talent shortage

It has a distribution problem. Talent concentrated in a few cities and a few company types isn't reaching the companies that need it.

Why SMBs are the next wave

Large tech companies over-hired and corrected. SMBs are absorbing that talent but lack the employer brand to find it directly.

The talent didn't disappear. It just stopped applying to job boards.
  • Look beyond traditional job boards for senior talent
  • SMB hiring requires different sourcing than enterprise
  • Speed matters more than brand recognition right now
